Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by entering your phone number on the login page and following the prompts.

How do I download my certificate?

Once you complete a course with 100% progress, visit the Certificates page and click the download button.

Can I access courses offline?

Currently, all courses require an internet connection. We're working on offline functionality for the future.

How long do I have access to a course?

You have lifetime access to all enrolled courses. Learn at your own pace!
